Traditional Kimchi Recipe

Description

Kimchi, is a traditional Korean banchan consisting of salted and fermented vegetables, so it is a fermented dish — the more it ages, the better it tastes.

If you love authentic Korean cuisine, try this classic kimchi recipe and make your own. It's not as complicated or time-consuming as you might think.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small radish, cubed
  • ¼ cup water, or as needed
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 small white onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • coarse salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 ripe persimmon, chopped
  • 3 medium green onions, minced
  • 1 head napa cabbage, shredded and rinsed
  • 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger
