Red Enchiladas Recipe

Description

This mouthwatering enchilada recipe takes you on a culinary journey through the vibrant flavors of Mexico. Whether you're a fan of Mexican cuisine or looking to explore new culinary horizons, these enchiladas are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more. Enjoy them as a satisfying meal for any occasion!

Ingredients:

  • 4 guajillo chiles, opened, cleaned, and seedless.
  • 4 ancho chiles, opened, cleaned, and seedless.
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced.
  • ¼ teaspoon of Mexican dried oregano.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • 12 corn tortillas.
  • 2 cups of shredded pork or chicken (optional).
  • 1 ½ cups of crumbled fresh cheese.
  • ½ cup of finely chopped white onion.
  • ⅓ cup of vegetable oil.
